Where do | get access to
my transcript?

Please speak with your
school's counselor or
registrar to get a copy of
your transcript. If you don't
have a school counselor,
please speak with your
school's administration
office.

Receiving a copy of your
transcript make take some
time. If you plan to apply to
one of the colleges that
requires Courses & Grades,
don't wait to ask!
